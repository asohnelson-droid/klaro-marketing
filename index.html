The reality

Running a business should not feel like guessing.

Every day, African entrepreneurs make high-stakes decisions without the information they need.

Did I actually make a profit today?
Revenue is visible. Real profit, after all expenses, is usually a guess.
Who still owes me money?
Credit is tracked in the head, or in a notebook that gets lost.
Which products are making me money?
Best sellers and highest-margin products are rarely the same thing.
Which customers have not returned?
Loyal customers drift away silently, and nobody notices until it is too late.
What should I do tomorrow?
No time to analyse. No advisor to call. Just instinct, under pressure.
